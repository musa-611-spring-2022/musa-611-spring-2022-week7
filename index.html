Migrant Deaths on the Arizona/Sonora Border

Those who cross the border into the US often underestimate the danger that awaits them on the other side before they reach civilization. As this tool illustrates, many perish in the desert of southern Arizona with the American dream right at their fingertips.

This website provides insight into the individuals who lost their lives attempting to cross the border through the Sonoran Desert. Data includes recorded deaths from 1987 to 2022
