Visits to urban independent pharmacies and large chain pharmacies are further polarized in 2021 due to the demand for vaccines and testing brought on by Covid-19. Data from SafeGraph, a mobility data company, was used to explore pharmacy visits. A score out of 100 is given, based on the number of visits recorded.
